The Cartoon

The Pokémon cartoon, shows the adventures of Ash Ketchum, a young trainer, and his trusty Pokémon, Pikachu, as they travel, make friends, face challenges, and compete in battles. The series is characterized by its message of friendship, teamwork and determination, and remains an iconic part of the global pop culture.

Oficial Game Pokemon Go

"Pokémon GO" is an augmented reality game released in 2016 by Niantic , in collaboration with the Pokémon Company and Nintendo. The game allows players to explore the real world in search and capture of Pokémon, using their cameras and GPS of mobile devices to superimpose the virtual creatures on the real environment.
